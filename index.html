The Land of Hills

Himachal Pradesh

A serene Himalayan state known for snow-capped peaks, pine forests, river valleys, temples, adventure trails, and warm local culture.

Quick Facts

  • Capital: Shimla
  • Famous for: Mountains and hill stations
  • Best known destinations: Manali, Dharamshala, Spiti

Top Places

Scenic destinations to visit

Shimla

The capital city offers colonial charm, Mall Road walks, and beautiful ridge views.

Manali

A popular mountain getaway with adventure sports, snowy peaks, and river scenery.

Dharamshala

Home to calm monasteries, tea gardens, and breathtaking views of the Dhauladhar range.

Spiti Valley

A dramatic cold desert landscape filled with remote villages and ancient monasteries.

Culture & Food

Traditions rooted in mountain life

Himachal Pradesh is rich in folk music, colorful festivals, wooden architecture, and local hospitality. Popular dishes include siddu, dham, madra, and trout.
